# Age_Calculator
# Age Calculator in Python  This program calculates your age based on your birthdate and a target date you choose.

Enter your birth year: 2000
Enter your birth month (1-12): 5
Enter the number of your birth day: 10
Enter the target year: 2030
Enter the target month (1-12): 1
Enter the target day: 1
Your age on 2030-01-01 will be: 29
